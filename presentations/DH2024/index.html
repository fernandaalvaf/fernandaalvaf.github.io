1. Context

The Zenon Archive

Detail and emphasis on the Barrington Atlas, no. 1.

Zenon's private and official businesses

  • Zenon emigrated from Caria to Egypt to work with the finance minister (Apollonios)
  • Follows the different phases of Zenon's career and his establishment in Egypt
  • Collection of documents pertaining the administration of a large estate in the Fayum
    •

The Texts of the Zenon Archive

The documents of the archive

  • Corpus with c. 1845 documents from the 3rd century BCE (subset: 886)
  • Letters, contracts, petitions, memoranda, etc.
  • Distributed in several collections in the world
  • The research focuses on the corpus of documents owned and published by the Cairo Museum (the P.Cair.Zen. volumes published by C.C. Edgar)
    •

2. Methodological Approach

Building a local database

  • Data collected from papyri.info and Trismegistos (web scraping script)
  • Collected data cleaned and stored on a local relational database managed with Microsoft Access
  • Data about the interpersonal interactions and relationships manually added through a GUI
  • Customized datasets could be exported to .csv for further processing with python and Gephi
    •

Relationships and Interactions

Interaction Relationship
  • isolated instance of engagement in mutual meaningful behaviors patterned by social norms, values and roles.
  • a financial transaction, a conversation, an order, etc.
  • characterized by ongoing interaction
  • implies the presence of some degree of interdependence (e.g., power relationships), mutual influence (e.g., professional relationships), and/or shared identity (e.g., familial or kinship relationships) or purpose (e.g., work relationships).
  • kinship, work, etc.
